My Goals

The goals area is divided into three sections: Personal, Academic and Career. Using the HTML editor within each box, you can create goal description artefacts. These may be added to your views.

Personal or career goals are often provided in résumés so that employers can understand how a particular role might suit your ambitions.

You can also use the academic goals section to describe achievements you are seeking in your programme of study.

Note: It is important to save each new entry using the button at the foot of the page.
