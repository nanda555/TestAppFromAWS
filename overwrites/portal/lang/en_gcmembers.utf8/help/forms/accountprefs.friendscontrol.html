Members Control

Choose whether other users may add you to their members list. If you are added to a members list, the owner of that list is added to your list. When you remove someone from your members list you are also removed from their list. Choose either:

Nobody may add me as a member: no one may add you to their list.

New members require my authorization: your authorization is needed for you to be added to a list. If you approve, they will be added to your list.

New members are automatically authorized: anyone may add you to their list.
