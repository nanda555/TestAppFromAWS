Activity Type

Select from this drop down menu the types of notifications you wish to view in your list.

System messages: automatically generated by the system or sent to you by one of the site administrators.

Messages from other users: sent to you directly from other users of the site.

Feedback: notification of any feedback or comments received on any views, artefacts or blogs within your ePortfolio.

Watchlist: notification of activity on any artefact, view, group or blog you are monitoring.

New views access: notification you or one of your groups have been given access to a new or existing non-public view.
